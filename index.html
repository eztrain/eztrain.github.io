Filling the Frame

Not to be confused with crowding the frame, filling the frame is simply giving the subject prominence inside the frame and thus greatly minimizing the impact of whatever are in the background. This composition is easily achieved and can create a strong visual impact.

crowding the frame

Frame your subject and isolate it from the rest. It can provide a visual boundary to a scene, thereby curbing the natural tendency of the eye to wander off the frame.
